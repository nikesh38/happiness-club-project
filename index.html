Essential Workouts for Students

Quick, effective exercises you can do anywhere, anytime!

Push-Ups

Duration: 3 sets × 10-15 reps

Build upper body strength and core stability. Perfect for dorm room workouts!

Squats

Duration: 3 sets × 15-20 reps

Strengthen legs and glutes. No equipment needed - just your body weight!

Plank Hold

Duration: 3 sets × 30-60 seconds

Core strengthening exercise. Start with 30 seconds and build up!

Jumping Jacks

Duration: 3 sets × 20-30 reps

Cardio boost to get your heart rate up. Great warm-up exercise!

Lunges

Duration: 3 sets × 10 reps each leg

Target legs and improve balance. Perfect for building lower body strength!

Burpees

Duration: 3 sets × 8-12 reps

Full-body workout combining strength and cardio. Maximum impact in minimal time!
